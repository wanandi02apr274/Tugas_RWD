BACKGROUND
Starting with a final project known as a thesis, arose an idea. In consideration of my goals in working on a thesis, making the thesis’ results useful for other people is one of the primary ones.
The issue at the time was anemia in pregnant women. On the account of the high number of maternal deaths from anemia, I wanted to create a product that anyone can enjoy without feeling like they are consuming drugs or supplements.
Moringa oleifera, also known as Miracle Tree in Europe and America, was also used by WHO (World Health Organization) to overcome nutritional problems in Africa, unfortunately its utilization is not as optimal in Indonesia. Subsequently, a business idea emerged to create Moringa-based products that could compete globally.
